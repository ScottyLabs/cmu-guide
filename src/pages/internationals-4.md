OIE has advisors who have appointments for meetings, should you need to do so, but they are almost always near-fully or fully booked. However, you can reach OIE by calling 412-268-5231 or emailing oie@andrew.cmu.edu and they are usually relatively timely with their responses. <-- TODO: make the phone number and email links

**You will be assigned a specific OIE advisor** whom you can contact for all of your OIE queries. They will be in charge of dealing with your visa status and all related concerns. You will be able to contact them via their own email/phone number.
